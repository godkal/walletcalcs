About WalletCalcs

WalletCalcs exists because most financial calculators online are either ad-choked shells that hide the answer, or oversimplified widgets that skip the inputs that actually change the result (property taxes that scale with price, employer match caps, the QBI deduction).

What we promise

  • The calculation is the product. Every tool computes locally in your browser — instant results, and your financial inputs never leave your device or touch our servers.
  • Assumptions in the open. Each calculator states what it includes, what it excludes, and which year's tax figures it uses.
  • No sign-up walls, no email capture, no spam.

What these tools are not

Educational estimates, not advice. Calculators can't see your full situation — a good CPA, CFP, or attorney can. For decisions with real stakes (buying a home, retiring, insurance), use these tools to arrive prepared, then verify with a professional.

Corrections

Found a number that looks wrong or a tax figure that's out of date? We genuinely want to know — open an issue or contact us and we'll verify against primary sources (IRS publications, agency guidelines) and fix it.
